FAQ

  • Does it work offline?
    Yes. Practice and tests work without internet; progress is stored on the device.
  • Where is my progress stored?
    All results are saved locally on the device. Removing the app deletes local data.
  • What statistics are available?
    Accuracy, average time per answer, streaks, and weak topics by multiplier.
  • How long is a practice session?
    Short sessions that typically take a few minutes, depending on speed.
  • How to contact support?
    Use the buttons above for email or Telegram. Include app version and device model.

Troubleshooting

  • No sound or haptics
    Check system mute and haptics settings, then restart the app.
  • App language
    Set the preferred language inside the app settings; restart if needed.
  • Crashes or freezes
    Update to the latest version and ensure enough free storage.
